## 3 Multi-Agent Systems
This section introduces the fundamental aspects of medical multi-agent systems.

### 3.1 Categories of Medical Multi-Agent Systems
Explore the various categories and types of medical multi-agent systems, highlighting their unique features and functionalities.

### 3.2 System Architecture
Dive into the architectural frameworks that support the implementation of multi-agent systems in healthcare environments.

### 3.3 Communication and Collaboration Mechanisms
Understand the communication strategies employed by medical agents to collaborate effectively within healthcare teams and between systems.

## 4 Atomic Capabilities of Medical Agents: A Functional Task-Level Perspective
This section focuses on the core functionalities that medical agents can perform.

### 4.1 Basic Technology Empowerment Function
Discuss the foundational technologies that enable medical agents to function.

### 4.2 Core Diagnostic and Therapeutic Assistance Function
Examine how medical agents assist in diagnostics and therapeutic processes.

### 4.3 Workflow and Documentation Optimization Function
Outline the ways in which medical agents streamline workflows and improve documentation practices.

### 4.4 Future Research Direction
Identify potential research areas to enhance the capabilities and integration of medical agents.

## 5 Medical Agents: The Hospital Department-level Practice Perspective
An overview of how multi-agent systems are applied across different hospital departments.

### 5.1 Neurology Department
Insights into the specific applications of medical agents within the neurology department.

### 5.2 Oncology Department
Explore the functionality and benefits of medical agents in oncology.

### 5.3 Pharmacy Department
Understand the role of medical agents in pharmacy operations.

### 5.4 Radiology Department
Investigate the applications of multi-agent systems in radiology.

### 5.5 Other Departments
Briefly cover the use of medical agents in other hospital departments.

### 5.6 Cross-departmental Applications
Evaluate how medical agents facilitate collaboration across different hospital departments.

## 6 Medical Agents: Applications Across Clinical Workflows and System Operations
This section delves into the applications of medical agents at various points in clinical workflows.

### 6.1 Early Stage of the Care Pathway: Intake and Clinical Dialogue
Detail the role of multi-agent systems in patient intake and communication.

### 6.2 Decision-Making at the Clinical Core: Virtual MDT Teams and Multimodal Reasoning
Examine how medical agents support decision-making in multi-disciplinary teams.

### 6.3 Treatment Procedures and Surgical Interventions
Discuss the integration of medical agents in surgical and treatment procedures.

### 6.4 From Therapy to Follow-up: Chronic Disease Management and Prescription Safety
Analyze the impact of medical agents on long-term patient management and prescription practices.

### 6.5 Making Data Usable: Documentation, Coding, and Knowledge Infrastructure
Highlight the technological contributions of medical agents to data management.

### 6.6 Simulation and Support Systems: For Education, Training and Trial
Explore how medical agents assist in training and simulation scenarios.

### 6.7 Regulation, Payer Workflows, and Administrative Automation
Discuss the role of medical agents in regulatory compliance and administrative tasks.

### 6.8 Open Issues and Our Next Steps
Identify ongoing challenges in the adoption of medical agents and outline future steps.

## 7 Safety of Medical Agents: Are We Ready to Trust AI with Patient Lives?
A critical examination of the safety considerations when integrating medical agents into patient care.

### 7.1 Medical Hallucination: Reasoning Failure and Clinical Misjudgment
Discuss the phenomena of medical hallucination and its implications for patient care.

### 7.2 Privacy and Data Security
Explore the importance of maintaining patient privacy and secure data practices.

### 7.3 Explainability and Transparency
Highlight the need for explainable AI in medical contexts.

### 7.4 Adversarial Security and Threat Modeling
Examine security concerns and potential threats to medical systems.

### 7.5 AI Governance and Systemic Safety
Discuss the frameworks needed for governance and safety in AI applications.

### 7.6 Bias, Fairness, and Accessibility
Address the ethical considerations of bias and accessibility in the deployment of medical agents.

## 8 How Should We Evaluate Medical Agents in Practice?
An overview of the evaluation methods for assessing medical agents.

### 8.1 Benchmarks
Identify standardized benchmarks for evaluating the performance of medical agents.

### 8.2 Metrics
Explore the metrics used to assess the effectiveness and efficiency of medical agents.

### 8.3 Challenge and Discussion
Engage in discussions about the challenges present in evaluating medical agents.

## 9 Open Challenges and Future Directions
Discuss the future landscape and challenges facing medical agents in healthcare.

### 9.1 Advancing Capabilities Under Clinical Constraints
Explore how to enhance medical agents’ capabilities while considering clinical constraints.

### 9.2 Trustworthy, Equitable, and Governed Medical Agents
Outline the importance of trustworthiness and equity in AI deployment.

### 9.3 Environments, Deployment, and Evaluation at System Scale
Discuss the need for scalable deployment and evaluation of medical agents.

### 9.4 Outlook
Provide a visionary perspective on the future of medical agents in healthcare.
